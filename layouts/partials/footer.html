About the Waccamaw Indian People

The Waccamaw Indian People are South Carolina's first state-recognized tribe (2005), with over 300 years of continuous presence in the Dog Bluff community near Aynor. We preserve our cultural heritage, maintain tribal sovereignty, and support our community through educational programs, cultural events, and traditional practices. Our annual Pauwau celebrates our living culture and welcomes visitors to experience authentic indigenous traditions.
