About Our Pauwau & Cultural Arts Festival

The Waccamaw Indian People host our annual Pauwau and Cultural Arts Festival during the first full weekend of November each year at the Waccamaw Tribal Grounds in Aynor, SC. This celebration welcomes visitors to experience authentic indigenous traditions in the heart of South Carolina's Lowcountry.

A Pauwau (also commonly spelled "powwow") is a vibrant gathering that celebrates Native American culture through traditional dancing, drumming, storytelling, and community fellowship. Our annual event showcases the rich heritage of the Waccamaw people while honoring intertribal connections and welcoming all who wish to learn about and respect indigenous traditions.
