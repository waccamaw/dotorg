Curriculum Alignment

School Day programming is designed to support South Carolina social studies and cultural education standards, including:

Elementary Standards

  • Native American cultures and traditions
  • South Carolina history and indigenous peoples
  • Cultural diversity and respect
  • Art, music, and storytelling traditions

Middle & High School Standards

  • Indigenous history and sovereignty
  • Cultural preservation and modern tribal life
  • Anthropology and archaeology
  • Social justice and indigenous rights

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register my school or class?

Registration will open in March {{ $currentYear }} through our online portal. Teachers and administrators can register entire classes, grade levels, or schools.

Can homeschool families attend?

Yes! Homeschool families and co-ops are welcome. Register through the School Day portal and indicate your homeschool group information.

What if my school can't attend Friday School Day?

School Day is specifically Friday only. However, the full Pauwau & Cultural Arts Festival weekend is open to all visitors. See the main Pauwau page for weekend details.

Are there age restrictions?

School Day is appropriate for all K-12 grade levels. Programming is designed to be engaging and age-appropriate for elementary through high school students.
