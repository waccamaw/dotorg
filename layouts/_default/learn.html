Our History & Living Culture

A living community in Horry County, South Carolina — with roots along these rivers that reach back long before the first Europeans arrived on this coast.

Whether you're a student meeting the Waccamaw for the first time, or tracing your own family's story back to the Dimery Settlement — welcome. This is a place to begin.
