# Pauwau 2026 - Vendor & Participant Registration

Thank you for your interest in participating in the Waccamaw Indian People's annual Pauwau celebration, held during the **first week of November 2026**.

## Registration Opens March 2026

Registration forms and applications for all participation opportunities will be available starting in **March 2026**. We encourage early registration to secure your preferred booth location or participation option.

## Participation Opportunities

### Vendor Booths

Set up a booth to sell handmade crafts, indigenous arts, traditional items, food, or other appropriate merchandise. Vendor booth applications include:

- Booth space dimensions and location preferences
- Required insurance and licensing documentation
- Product descriptions and pricing guidelines
- Setup and teardown instructions

**Registration Form:** Available March 2026

---

### Dance Raffles

Participate in traditional dance raffles and cultural performances during the Pauwau. Registration includes:

- Dance style and regalia information
- Performance schedule preferences
- Raffle entry requirements
- Prize and award details

**Registration Form:** Available March 2026

---

### RV Parking & Camping

Reserve RV parking or camping space for multi-day attendance. RV registration includes:

- Space reservation and hookup options
- Duration of stay (overnight/multi-day)
- Facility access and amenities
- Parking fees and payment schedules

**Registration Form:** Available March 2026

---

### Additional Opportunities

Additional participation options may include:

- Food vendor permits
- Demonstration and workshop spaces
- Sponsorship opportunities
- Volunteer positions

Details and registration forms will be announced in March 2026.

## Important Dates

- **March 2026** - Registration opens
- **July 2026** - Early registration deadline (recommended)
- **October 2026** - Final registration deadline
- **First week of November 2026** - Pauwau celebration

## Application Process

When registration opens in March 2026, each participation option will have a dedicated online application form powered by Zeffy (a zero-fee fundraising platform). You'll be able to:

1. Complete your application online
2. Upload required documentation (DoI forms, insurance, etc.)
3. Pay applicable fees securely
4. Receive confirmation and additional details via email
